# Presentations

Lab presentations from the Monash Robotics Weekly Meeting and Robotics Reading Group.

**Meetings:**

- **Monash Robotics Weekly Meeting** — Thursdays 10–11am AEST, in-person (18 Alliance Ln, Clayton, G-G15-Lounge) + Zoom. Skill-sharing and research presentations.
- **Robotics Reading Group** — Thursdays 1–2pm AEST, same location + Zoom. Paper reading presentations.

To attend, contact Dana Kulić (`dana.kulic@monash.edu`) to request a calendar invite.

To add your slides, see [Contributing → Sharing Presentations](contributing.md#sharing-presentations).

!!! note
    Unpublished research presentations are in a private repo accessible to org members only and are not listed here.

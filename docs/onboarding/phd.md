# New PhD Student Checklist

Getting started in the HRI group at Monash Robotics.

## Community

- Join Slack at [monashrobotics.slack.com](https://monashrobotics.slack.com)
- Request access to relevant GitHub repositories (MonashRobotics or hri-group)
- Get added to the [Monash Robotics website](https://www.monash.edu/engineering/robotics) — contact Tina Wu

## Required Training & Access

- Complete OHS (Occupational Health & Safety) lab induction
- Gain access to meeting rooms through departmental admin
- Request lab equipment training as needed

## Regular Meetings

| Meeting | Time | Format |

| HRI Group weekly | Thursday 10–11am | Zoom |
| Reading group | Wednesday 11am–12pm | Paper discussions |
